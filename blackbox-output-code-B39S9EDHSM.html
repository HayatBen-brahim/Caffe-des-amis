Our Story

☕

At Caffè Les Amis, we bring the rich flavors of Morocco to your cup. Our coffee is roasted with traditional methods passed down through generations, blended with aromatic spices that awaken your senses.

From buttery msemen to delicate chebakia, our pastries are made fresh daily using authentic Moroccan recipes. Come relax in our cozy atmosphere and let us be your friends (les amis) for the perfect coffee break.
